    Whisky pricing: A dram good case study

    In association with TheWhiskyExchange (TWE)

  • Is the price of a whisky right? As a collector and a hobbyist, I set out to explore the factors that affect the price of a whisky. Is it the taste, is it the marketing, is it the looks?
  • The challenges in this project were EDA & deductive imputation. Majority of the time was spent filling NaN values, EDA and understading meaning from test
  • Since I needed a model that was interpretable by TWE, I used Logistic Regression as my base model and used GridSearchCV to hyperparameterize it. The end result was excellent and provided insight into the differences between a value brand vs a ultra premium brand.
  • Finally, with all these recommendations, I was able to conclude that distillery info, vintage, flavors, type of whisky etc are responsible for the price.
